I work as a Research Assistant at IHSAN Lab, Information Technology University (ITU), Lahore, Pakistan. My research interests are diverse: I want to make sure that Artificial Intelligence (AI) is used responsibly. I am also interested in robust media generation using AI (preferably using few shot learning). I am also want to enable ML as a product in real-world settings (e.g., ML as a service (supporting encryption standards/protocols), ML on edge).

My past research work has largely focused on the trustworthy Machine Learning (ML) algorithms, particularly Deep Neural Networks (DNNs). In my research works, trustworthiness refers to the adversarial robustness, security, privacy, interpretability, alignment and fairness of DNNs.
